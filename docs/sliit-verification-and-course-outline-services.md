## Academic Level and Qualification Verification (SLIIT Programs)

This section applies to students who have followed SLIIT diplomas and degrees.

### Where to send requests

External parties requesting academic level/qualification verification should email:

- `studentservices@sliit.lk`

### Required documents

Include all of the following:

1. Candidate details being verified
2. Clear scanned copies of required verification documents
3. Payment receipt with transaction ID

### Verification service charges

- Local verifications: `LKR 5,500/-` (from January 2025 onwards)
- Foreign verifications (SLIIT programs only): `LKR 13,500/-` (LKR)

### Payment instructions

- Payment should be made in favor of `SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY`.
- Use the SLIIT student's registration number as the transaction ID.
- Internet Payment Gateway (SLIIT IPG) for VISA/Master cards:
  - https://pay.sliit.lk
- Bank charges may apply.

### Processing and delivery

- Once request + payment slip are received, verification documents are sent within `5 to 7 working days`.

### Important note on document integrity

- Candidates submitting forged documents will be reported to the nearest Sri Lankan police, Sri Lankan consulate, or embassy according to institute policy and applicable laws.

### Franchised program document verification

For foreign degree certificate/transcript verification, students should use the partner institute services:

- LJMU graduates: https://buyonline.ljmu.ac.uk/product-catalogue/ljmu/student-administration/document-checking-services
- Curtin graduates: https://students.curtin.edu.au/essentials/graduation/forms-documents/online-award-verification/
- SHU graduates: https://store.shu.ac.uk/product-catalogue/central-services/course-documents/award-verification-letter

## Credit Conversions to European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System (ECTS)

Students are advised to refer to Quality Assurance Council (UGC) guidelines for credit conversions:

- https://www.eugc.ac.lk/qac/slqf-add-faq.html#:~:text=Based%20on%20the%20information%20given,credits%20and%205%20UK%20credits.

### General conversion guidance (illustrative)

- SLQF defines `1 credit = 50 notional hours`
- ECTS defines `1 credit = 25 to 30 hours`
- If ECTS 1 credit is considered 25 hours, then:
  - `1 SLQF credit ~= 2 ECTS credits`
- UK credit system:
  - `1 UK credit = 10 hours`
  - `1 SLQF credit ~= 5 UK credits` and approximately `2 ECTS credits`

Additional references:

- https://www.qaa.ac.uk/the-quality-code/higher-education-credit-framework-for-england
- https://www.qaa.ac.uk/docs/qaa/quality-code/what-is-credit-guide-for-students.pdf

This information is for general guidance only. For confirmed equivalency, contact the relevant quality assurance agencies in the target country.

## Issuance of Course Outlines for Overseas University Applications

SLIIT facilitates issuance of module/course outlines to support overseas university and higher-education applications. SLIIT issues the official syllabus directly to the requested university.

### Who should request how

- Current students:
  - Submit a Support Desk ticket: https://support.sliit.lk/
  - Path: `Request/Inquiry type -> Official Documents -> Course Outline`
- Graduated (pass-out) students:
  - Email: `studentservices@sliit.lk`

### Application procedure

To request a course outline:

1. Complete the formal request form:
   - https://support.sliit.lk/downloads/files/request-form-course-outline
2. Attach:
   - Scanned copy of transcript
   - Scanned copy of certificate
   - Proof of payment (bank transfer slip):
     - https://support.sliit.lk/downloads/files/new-bank-fund-transer-details-sliit

### Service details and fees

- Digital copy (normal service): `Rs. 9,500/-`
- Processing time: `7 to 10 working days`

### Delivery and notification

- Once the complete request (form + supporting documents + payment slip) is received, the course outline is sent directly to the relevant overseas university within `7 to 10 working days`.
- Email confirmation is sent after dispatch.

For updates, contact:

- Mr. Kenneth Flemming - `kenneth.f@sliit.lk`
- Ms. Yasodha Rathnayake - `yasodha.r@sliit.lk` (International Programmes Unit)

For further assistance, contact the International Programmes Unit at SLIIT.
