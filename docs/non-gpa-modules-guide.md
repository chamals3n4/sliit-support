# Non-GPA modules

What are the non-GPA modules in Faculty of Computing, SLIIT
Business School/Faculty of Engineering, and Faculty of Humanities
and Sciences?

Non-GPA modules are modules components of the academic program that do not contribute to the calculation of
a student's Grade Point Average (GPA).

Sometimes a specific study program of a Faculty might make such Non-GPA modules compulsory as a
graduation requirement.

However, students who are registered to these modules are required to attempt these modules and obtain a pass
mark to be eligible for graduation. If a student needs to repeat a non-GPA module, they must register for it
under the prorated module registration category.
