# SLIIT Payment Methods and Bank Transfer Details

## How to Pay?

Payments should be made in favour of "SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY" at the following banks. Please indicate your REGISTRATION NUMBER as the Transaction ID.

---

## Available Student Payment Methods

### Internet Payment Gateway (IPG)

**VISA and Master Debit or Credit Cards**

- Portal: https://pay.sliit.lk
- Note: Bank charges will be applicable

**AMEX Cards**

- Portal: https://www.nationstrust.com/nationspay/

## Payment Methods by Type

### Cash Deposits

Deposits can be made at the following bank branches:

- Bank of Ceylon, Kollupitiya Super Grade Branch
- Sampath Bank, Malabe Branch
- Nations Trust Bank, Malabe Branch
- Hatton National Bank, Malabe Branch

### Online Fund Transfers

**Sampath Bank – Vishwa Online**

- Use SLIIT Payment Interface under "Higher Education Institutes"

**Bank of Ceylon – Online Fund Transfers**

- Add SLIIT as a beneficiary and perform payment through the Interface under "Schools"

**Hatton National Bank – Online Fund Transfers**

- Add SLIIT as a beneficiary and perform payment through the Interface under "Education"
- Can also use NTB Fri Mi App with SLIIT Payment Interface under "Education"

### NTB CDM Machine Deposits

- CDM Machine located in SLIIT Campus, Malabe
- Note: Please do not use any other CDM machines as deposits cannot be separately identified to your registration number
- Select SLIIT Payment Interface at the machine

### Direct Remittances

Foreign students can transfer funds to SLIIT account through direct remittances:

- USD Account No: 270380000468 at NTB, Malabe Branch
